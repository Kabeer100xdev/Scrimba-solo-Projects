Top three activities to do at Earth

Jump in Mud Puddles

Water falls from the sky, makes squishy mud. Jump in it, go splash! Humans say it's messy, but fun!

Try Ice Cream

It’s cold, it’s sweet, it melts on your face. Earth kids love it! (Don’t eat too much or you’ll feel weird.)

Play Hide-and-Seek with Humans

Humans close their eyes and count; you hide! If they find you, say "Boo!" and watch them scream!
